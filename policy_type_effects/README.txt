This folder contains data and scripts used in analyses of the effects of US federal policies on housing outcomes by type, 
and related effects on residential energy demand and greenhouse gas emissions.
The results generated by these scripts are used for the paper in Environmental Science and Technology:
"Linking Housing Policy, Housing Typology, and Residential Energy Demand in the United States" by
Peter Berrill, Kenneth T. Gillingham, and Edgar G. Hertwich

doi: https://dx.doi.org/10.1021/acs.est.0c05696
